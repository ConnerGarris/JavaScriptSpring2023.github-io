Tips To Browse Safely

The first and best thing you can do to browse safely is to turn on the strictest privacy settings in your browser settings, this prevents people from pulling certain sensitive information like this page has. Source

The next thing you can do is to install cross-site tracker bockers that prevent advertisers from tracking you between multiple websites and building a profile for what you like and who you are. Source 2

HTTP is unencrypted, while HTTPS is secure due to the SSL encryption. It's important to look for the "HTTPS" and lock symbol in the URL to ensure a secure connection, as browsers transmit information through HTTPS automatically. Source 3
