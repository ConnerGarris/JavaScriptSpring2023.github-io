Mike Herrington

Mike is a true coffee enthusiast who turned his passion into a business. With a deep love for the art and science of coffee brewing, Mike started this coffee shop as his passion project to share his knowledge and expertise with the local community. He sources only the highest quality coffee beans, roasting them to perfection and experimenting with different brewing techniques to create unique and delicious flavors. Mike is a warm and welcoming presence in the shop, always happy to chat with customers and share his passion for coffee with anyone who walks through the door. His dedication to his craft is evident in every cup of coffee served, and his shop has become a beloved gathering place for coffee lovers in the area.

Benny Yusufoglu

Benny is a co-owner of a coffee shop who brings a unique perspective to the business. Originally from Germany, he has a deep appreciation for European coffee culture, which has inspired some of the elements of the shop. Benny is passionate about coffee and is constantly seeking out the best beans to create the perfect cup. He has a meticulous attention to detail and is always experimenting with new brewing techniques to create exceptional drinks.

Lord Uudtrich

The coffee shop is lucky enough to have an adorable furry friend named Lord Uudtrich, who has become a beloved and integral part of the community. Lord Uudtrich, a friendly and well-behaved pup, is always eager to greet every customer with a wagging tail and a big smile. As customers sip their coffee and catch up with friends, Lord Uudtrich can often be found wandering around the shop, eagerly accepting pets and belly rubs from anyone who offers. His presence adds to the warm and welcoming atmosphere of the shop, and many regulars stop by specifically to see their furry friend, Lord Uudtrich. The coffee shop is not just a place to enjoy a great cup of coffee, but also a place where customers can connect with each other and with the community, thanks in no small part to the charming and lovable Lord Uudtrich who calls the shop home.
